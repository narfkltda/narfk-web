Frequently asked questions

Do I need an account?

No account required! iBibleVerse works completely offline and stores all your data locally on your device. Your privacy is our priority.

What Bible versions are supported?

iBibleVerse supports multiple Bible translations including NIV, ESV, KJV, and more. You can switch between versions to compare different translations.

Can I track my progress?

Absolutely! iBibleVerse offers comprehensive progress tracking to help you monitor your learning journey, quiz scores, and study achievements.

Which devices are supported?

iBibleVerse is designed for iOS devices running iOS 15.0 or later. It's optimized for iPhone and works great on iPad too.
