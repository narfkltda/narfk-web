Privacy Policy

Last updated: September 2025

Welcome to iBibleVerse. Your privacy matters and we are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, store, and share your information, as well as your rights regarding it.

⸻

1. Definitions

  • Personal Data: any information that can identify you individually (e.g., name, email address).
  • Usage Data: non-identifying data about how you interact with the app (e.g., how often you open features, crash reports).
  • Tracking: the act of linking personal data or usage across apps or websites to deliver personalized ads or analytics.

⸻

2. Data We Collect

  • We collect Usage Data whether or not you give consent via Apple's App Tracking Transparency (ATT).
  • No Personal Data is collected without your explicit consent. Specifically, we do NOT collect: your name, location, contacts, financial information etc.

⸻

3. Local Data & Security

  • All quiz scores, progress, notes, and bookmarks are stored directly on your device.
  • Data is protected by iOS sandboxing and secure storage mechanisms (e.g., Keychain or local file encryption where applicable).
  • We do not upload your data to external servers without your permission.

⸻

4. Data Export

  • You may export your data (quiz results, progress etc.) in CSV, TXT or PDF formats.
  • These exported files may contain sensitive information; please handle them securely.
  • After export, you are responsible for deleting or safeguarding these files.

⸻

5. Tracking & Advertising

  • We adhere fully to Apple's ATT framework. Any tracking (for ads or analytics) is optional and only performed if you permit it.
  • We use tools such as Firebase (for crash reports, performance analytics) and AdMob (for ads) in compliance with their privacy standards.
  • We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties for advertising purposes.

⸻

6. In-App Purchases & Payment Information

  • All subscriptions and purchases are processed via Apple's App Store.
  • We do not handle or store your payment information.

⸻

7. Retention & International Data Transfer

  • Usage logs and crash reports are retained only for as long as needed to improve the app's performance and fix issues.
  • If any third-party services store data outside your region/country, we ensure they comply with applicable data protection regulations.

⸻

8. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you have rights to:

  • Access the data we hold about you.
  • Correct inaccurate data.
  • Request deletion of your data.
  • Withdraw consent for tracking or analytics.
  • Portability of your exported data.

If you wish to exercise these rights, contact us via email below.

⸻

9. Changes to this Policy

  • This policy may be updated occasionally.
  • We will notify users of significant changes via in-app notice or via the app's listing page.
  • The "Last updated" date will reflect when changes take effect.

⸻

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions or requests about this policy:

Email: contact@narfk.com

Website: https://narfk.com/ibibleverse
