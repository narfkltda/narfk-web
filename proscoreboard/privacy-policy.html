Privacy Policy

Last updated: September, 2025

Thank you for using ProScoreboard. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our app. ProScoreboard is designed with a privacy-first philosophy, ensuring your data stays secure, local, and fully under your control.

• 1. Data Collection

Most of your data — including game scores, team statistics, match history, and custom settings — is stored locally on your device using secure storage mechanisms.

We do not ask for or store:

  • Personal identifiers (e.g., name, email)
  • Location data
  • Contacts
  • Financial accounts

• 2. Local Data & Encryption

All user data is stored locally on your device using Apple's secure storage frameworks. No data is ever uploaded to external servers without your consent.

You are the sole owner of your game data and team information.

• 3. Data Export

ProScoreboard allows exporting game data, team statistics, and match history in CSV, TXT, and PDF formats. These files may contain sensitive team and player information.

We recommend exporting only on trusted devices and deleting files when no longer needed.

• 4. App Tracking Transparency (ATT)

We comply with Apple's ATT framework. Tracking permissions are optional and only requested for ads and analytics.

• 5. Analytics & Advertising

ProScoreboard uses:

  • Google Analytics – for anonymous usage analytics.
  • Firebase – for crash reporting and performance monitoring.
  • AdMob – to display relevant ads (non-intrusive, compliant with Apple and Google policies).

We do not sell or share personal information.

• 6. In-App Purchases

Handled 100% via Apple's App Store. We do not process or store any payment information.

• 7. Your Rights

Since ProScoreboard stores data locally:

  • We cannot access or delete your data remotely.
  • You may delete your data by uninstalling the app.
  • If you export your data, you are responsible for its storage and privacy.
