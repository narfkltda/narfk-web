Frequently asked questions

Which sports are supported?

ProScoreboard supports basketball, football, soccer, hockey, volleyball, tennis, and many other sports. You can also create custom scoring rules for unique games or competitions.

Can I export game data?

Yes! ProScoreboard allows you to export game statistics, team performance data, and match history in various formats for analysis and record keeping.

Does it work offline?

Absolutely! ProScoreboard works completely offline, so you can use it anywhere without an internet connection. All your data is stored locally on your device.

Can I use it on multiple devices?

ProScoreboard is designed for iOS devices. You can install it on multiple iOS devices and transfer data between them using iCloud or manual export/import features.
