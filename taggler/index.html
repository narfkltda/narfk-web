Frequently asked questions

Do I need an account?

No account required! Taggler works completely offline and stores all your data locally on your device. Your privacy is our priority.

How does exporting work?

Pro users can export their data in multiple formats (CSV, TXT, PDF) directly from the app. Your data stays under your control at all times.

Can I filter by tag/date/type/status?

Absolutely! Taggler offers powerful filtering options to help you analyze your spending patterns by any combination of tags, dates, transaction types, and more.

Which devices are supported?

Taggler is designed for iOS devices running iOS 15.0 or later. It's optimized for iPhone and works great on iPad too.
